3D Printing Quality Expectations

We believe transparency is the best policy. While we would like all our products to be 100% spotless. 3D printed products produce unique features and heres what you can expect.

Layer lines

AM (Additive Manufacturing) is a technique in which material is deposited in layers to create a 3D object, as a result layer lines are a natural occurance. We typically use ≤ 0.2mm layer height to produce a smooth and aesthetically pleasing print

Seams

Small marks apear on some edges. caused by the toolhead of the printer moving up to the next layer. Although we hide them as best as possible they are a natural occurance for all 3D printed products.

Dimensional accuracy

Our printers are tuned and calibrated to produce dimensionally accurate prints. Although it would be ideal to have atomic accuracy, 3D printing isnt the most accuracy manufacturing method. However, we will accept a calibration value of less than 0.05mm variance for each axis (x,y,z).

Stringing

Known also as oozing or whiskers, occurs when small strings of plastic are left on the print. This is caused by plastic oozing out of the nozzle while moving to a new location. We tune all our filament to produce little to no stringing. If there are any small amounts left, they are easily removed with your hand, or a sharp blade.
